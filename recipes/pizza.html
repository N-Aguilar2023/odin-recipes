Pizza recipe

Pizza is a traditional Italian dish that consists of a dough base, usually topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and other ingredients such as meats, vegetables, and spices. It is known for its versatility and popularity around the world.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/4 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup sliced onions
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  5. Add the sliced pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, and onions on top of the cheese.
  6. Sprinkle the dried oregano and basil over the toppings.
  7. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  8. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
