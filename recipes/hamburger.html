Hamburger recipe

A hamburger is a classic American dish consisting of a cooked patty of ground meat, usually beef, placed inside a sliced bread roll or bun. It is often garnished with various toppings such as lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, cheese, and condiments like ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise. Hamburgers are a popular fast food item and can be enjoyed in a variety of ways, from simple and traditional to gourmet and elaborate.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 hamburger bun
  • 1 slice of cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Pickles
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Mayonnaise
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat your grill or stovetop pan to medium-high heat.
  2. Season the ground beef with salt and pepper, then form it into a patty.
  3. Cook the patty on the grill or pan for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until it reaches your desired level of doneness.
  4. Toast the hamburger bun on the grill or in a toaster.
  5. Place the cooked patty on the bottom half of the bun.
  6. Add a slice of cheese on top of the patty.
  7. Layer lettuce, tomato, onion, and pickles on top of the cheese.
  8. Spread ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise on the top half of the bun.
  9. Place the top half of the bun on the assembled ingredients.
  10. Serve and enjoy your homemade hamburger!
